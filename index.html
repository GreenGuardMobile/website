Services

GreenGuard Mobile is an application designed for precise detection of plant parasites and diseases. It offers advanced features such as recording user-captured histories in local storage and a built-in gallery function, enabling users to easily browse and view previously captured images. Available on both iOS and Android platforms, GreenGuard provides a comprehensive solution for monitoring and managing plant health issues.

Detection & Identification

GreenGuard Mobile accurately identifies plant parasites and diseases through image analysis, providing users with detailed information about the detected issues.

Integrated Gallery

Users can conveniently browse and view previously captured images within the app's built-in gallery, facilitating easy reference and comparison.

History Tracking

The app allows users to store and manage a history of captured images locally, enabling them to track the progression of plant health issues over time.

Cross-Platform Compatibility

GreenGuard Mobile is compatible with both iOS and Android devices, ensuring accessibility for a wide range of users across different mobile platforms.

Sponsors

GreenGuard Mobile is proudly sponsored by Ibn Tofail University in Kenitra and Fablabs, fostering innovation and research in the agricultural sector.
